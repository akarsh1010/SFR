Gateway to Pilgrimage

Your base for Char Dham Yatra

Strategically located in Barkot, we offer easy access to the four sacred abodes. Let us help you with transportation & yatra guidance.

Yamunotri

Source of the Yamuna River & sacred temple dedicated to Goddess Yamuna. Starting point of the divine journey.

~42 km from resort

Gangotri

Origin of the holy Ganga river, where Goddess Ganga descended. Mesmerizing high-altitude temple.

~175 km

Kedarnath

One of the 12 Jyotirlingas, dedicated to Lord Shiva. A profound spiritual experience amidst the Himalayas.

~210 km

Badrinath

Sacred abode of Lord Vishnu, situated on the banks of Alaknanda river — vibrant and divine.

~245 km
